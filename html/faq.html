Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make a reservation?

To make a reservation, visit "Book Now" section, from there you can fill up the form.

Can I cancel or change my booking?

Yes, you can cancel or change your booking two days before you travel.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all types of online payments, Visa and Mastercard and bank transfer.

What documents do I need for my trip?

To travel with us you need to provide your national identity card, two copies of your photo and mobile number. Please contact us to know more.

How do I contact customer service?

To contact customer service, visit our travel website and look for the "contact me" section
